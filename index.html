About the Contest

Data sheets for any Electrical/Electronic component, device or appliance may seem lengthy and complicated at first glance, but for an experienced design engineer they contain the critical component information necessary for successful design. It’s very necessary to properly understand the data sheet details which will ultimately reduce the risk of mistakes while carrying out any project work.

Students of EE/EC should be familiar with behavioral details of basic components like Resistor, Inductor, Capacitor, Diode, Transistor, IC’s, Transformers, Electrical Machines etc. It is expected that they correctly interpret the details given in sheet and can appropriately select/use the component for the said purpose.

The competition is designed to examine students awareness and ability in understanding the given data sheet.

Quiz Round


Participants will be tested for fundamental engineering knowledge of Electrical & Electronics which includes Analog/Digital Electronics, Network Analysis, Electrical Machines etc. The participants are required to complete the quiz in given time frame. Shortlisted teams will be promoted to Datasheet Inference Round.

Datasheet Inference Round


Expect the unexpected in this round. Participants will be examined towards basic know how about datasheet in first take. And then Data sheet inference skills.

General Rules

  • The whole activity will be conducted online.
  • The contest is open for EE and EC (1 st -4 th Year) students of Engineering colleges.
  • It is a Team event and maximum TWO numbers of participants per team.
  • The team members must be from the same college; however there is no restriction on the semester selection. i.e, a student from 2nd sem can team up with 6th sem)

Quiz Round rules

  • Quiz is designed for 30 Marks comprising of 1 and 2 mark questions.
  • The top scoring teams will qualify for the next round. (Top-10).
  • Participants need to attempt quiz by operating "Take quiz" section of the website
  • The Time limit for the quiz is 40 minutes.

Datasheet Infererence Round Rules

  • All the participants are requested to download the Google Meet application on their devices and be conversant with its use.
  • The Google Meet link for the event will be updated on results section of the website.
  • All the participants are requested to ensure the internet availability throughout the duration of event.
  • Phase 1: Data sheet of Basic components will be given to the participants and they should narrate the document.
  • Phase 2: Datasheet-2 will be provided; Participants have to read the datasheet, analyze it and be prepared for the question/answer round.
  • The preparation time for the datasheet analysis is 15 min. The participants are then expected to describe the details of the datasheet within 15 min. The Judges will then pose their questions.
