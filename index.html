About the Contest

The contest is designed to test the Electrical and Electronics knowledge of participants. Contest is conducted in two phases. The first phase is a filtration round which consists of on online quiz and second phase consists of datasheet inference and explanation.

Quiz Round


This is a online quiz round for filtration purspose. Participants are tested on their knowledge about Analog circuits, Logic design, transformers, machines etc. Quiz has 2 sections. The participants are required to complete both the sections of quiz in given time frame.

Datasheet Inference Round


This is the final round. Participants are expected to know basic datasheet inference skills. Expect the unexpected in this round.

General Rules

  • All the rounds will be conducted Online.
  • The Contest is open for Students in other colleges as well.
  • It is a Team event and maximum number of participants per team is 2.
  • The Team members must be from the same college and there is no restriction about
    the semesters in which they are in i.e, a student from 2nd sem can form a team with 6th sem as well.
  • Participants are requested to join the WhatsApp group after registering for the contest.
  • The contest is open for students from 1st, 2nd and 3rd year.
  • This contest is open to all who belong to ECE and EEE departments.

Quiz Round rules

  • The quiz will be conducted online.
  • Participation in the quiz is free and open to all who belong to ECE and EEE departments.
  • Quiz is designed for 30 Marks comprising of 1 and 2 mark questions.
  • The top scoring teams will qualify for the next round. (Top-10).
  • Participants need to attempt quiz by going to "Take quiz" section of the website
  • The quiz link will be closed after 4 hours, participants have to answer the quiz within this time duration.
  • The Time limit for the quiz is 1 hour.

Datasheet Infererence Round Rules

  • All the participants are requested to download the Google Meet application on their devices and be conversant with its use.
  • The Google Meet link for the event will be updated in results section of the website.
  • All the participants are requested to ensure the internet availability throughout the duration of event as no concession of any sort will be granted on this ground.
  • This Round is splitted into two phases.(Top-10).
  • Initially a Generalized topic will be given to the participants and then the participants will have to find a datasheet on this and then present it.
  • In the second phase, Datasheet will be provided to the participants. Participants have to read the datasheet, analyze it and be prepared for the question/answer round.
  • The preparation time for the datasheet analysis is 15 min. The participants are then expected to describe the details of the datasheet within 15 min. The Judges will then pose their questions.
  • The presenters must wear the identity cards issued by their respective colleges all the times during the event.
